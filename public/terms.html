Honest limits

OrionPDF is upfront about what it can't yet do:

  • Password-protected or encrypted PDFs cannot currently be opened or created.
  • "Edit Text" and "Find & Replace" work by covering the original text and placing new text over it — the file is not re-typeset.
  • Very large PDFs (500 MB+) are limited by your browser's available memory.
  • License validation is client-side deterrence, not copy protection — appropriate for a low-cost product, but not unbreakable DRM.
