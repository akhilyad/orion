Documentation

Open a PDF (File → Open, Ctrl+O, or drag & drop). Your first document is a free try with the core tools (view, annotate, organize pages); Premium (€1) unlocks everything, unlimited.

Annotate with the toolbar: text, highlight, draw, shapes, arrows. Select (V) to move things; double-click text to edit it in place; Eraser (E) or Delete removes.

Add Text (A): a box follows your cursor — click to anchor it, type directly on the page, drag the dotted border to move it. Enter commits (Ctrl+Enter for multi-line).

Edit Text (T) shows dotted boxes around the page's text — click one and retype it right on the page. Select Text (I) drag-selects page text for copying (Ctrl+C). Find & Replace (Ctrl+H) rewrites across the document.

Organize: drag thumbnails to reorder; rotate, delete, duplicate, insert and extract pages from the toolbar, sidebar or right-click menu.

Sign & Stamp: draw a signature (S) or place an APPROVED/DRAFT stamp, then click the page.

Export (Ctrl+S) burns everything into a real PDF — nothing ever leaves your device.
